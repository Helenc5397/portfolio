AI, Robots, and Circuits

Today, we talked a lot about robots. Here are some questions that we have to answer:

We talked a lot about robots and their ethics today. How did your idea of robotics change? What makes you excited about the future of robotics? Nervous?

My idea of a robot did not change that much, though it has added another possibility when I saw the wooden “robots” that works when wind blows on it. I am very excited about the future of robotics especially in the realm of artificial intelligence. I feel nervous about military drones though. One thing that I am concerned about is that the distance between the initiator and the target will allow the person in control of the drone and making decisions to kill to be more comfortable with killing because he/she does not have to deal with death face to face and this can lead to an abuse of power especially with drones which, unlike ballistic missiles, can be easily taken out for use without repeated security measures and time to consider.

You worked on circuits in groups, and will be working in groups, and will be working in groups for the rest of the week. What worked well? What could you do better next time?

It worked very well for us. Everyone was able to participate, and we accomplish the task quickly and easily. Something that we could do better next time would be to understand why the system work.
